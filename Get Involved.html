The Club made a modest start, with initial 30 members, at the time of applying for charter. We believe that club members groom the clubs culture and its growth. In tune, with RI directives – the club aims at enrolling younger members and lady members as well. It’s vital to ensure healthy and modest growth for sustainability. The club has laid its objective to increase its membership by at-least five per year over the next three years. Despite some members who discontinued owing to personal reasons, new members have been added and the process of new member induction continues.
Project Positive Health, is a PAN India initiative, which endeavors to work upon the cause of preventing and controlling the extent of Non-Communicable Diseases. As most are aware, NCDs are by far, predominantly life-threating diseases. This program is being actively promoted by RID 3131. Program was earlier initiated in Rotary by RI Directors, Dr. Bharat Pandya and Rtn. Kamal Sanghavi and furthered by National Committee Member, Rtn. Dr. Girish Gune, who added an important tag line to this “Ek Chamach Kam Chaar Kadam Aage”. The message is loud and clear to reduce the daily intake of Salt, Sugar and Oil by at-least one spoon and exercise regularly.
Whereas, the process of executing this project is well-defined, RCP Vibrant East, takes pride to announce that we have attempted to lead this project in an innovative manner by way of developing a Mobile App.
Towards our efforts to promote and showcase the concept, purpose and importance of PPH, we have already designed some presentations, which are as listed below. These are in the form of PowerPoint presentations and videos, developed for Rotary Clubs and public at large. They are creatively developed to provide an explanation of PPH, Features & Functionality of PPH App etc. We are also in process of developing more presentations and promotion material.
All technical support and expertise in respect of the App is offered by Rotary Club of Pune Vibrant East. You may post your suggestions, feedback and queries to us on pph@rcpve.org.

The App offers advocacy for improving the general health and well-being of the individual user, through regular monitoring of health vitals. Rotaract clubs are the building blocks towards grooming young professionals – Rotaractors into Rotary. Rotaract Club of Vibrant East is the first community based which is established in year 2018-19. Plans are afoot to start a new Rotaract clubs in near future. Rotaractors are encouraged to participate in various important programs within Rotary such a RYLA, RYE for their own self-development and enhancing their leadership qualities. We also encourage Rotaract Club members to associate with the Interact Clubs for knowledge sharing and better bonding.
Impactful and sustainable projects need to be showcased under Rotary Image. Public Image activities are essential to attract like-minded professionals towards Rotary. Every year we plan to do at-least three to four such projects. Some of the PI activities done earlier include a video that was circulated in Rotary on the subject “Brisk Walking to Prevent Heart Diseases”, A Musical Event – “Zindagi ka Safar”, Slow Cycling for age group above 40 etc.
Despite the shift in Rotary’s image that it be seen more as a member organization than a service organization, Service projects are the foundation of Rotary. Rotary defines its five areas of service which include – Club Service, Vocational Service, Community Service, Youth Service and International Service. To this end, service projects encompass the direst needs of today’s most critical issues, such as children at risk, disabled persons, health care, international understanding and goodwill, literacy and numeracy, population issues, poverty and hunger, the environment, clean water, and Polio eradication. Projects also support programs for the youth, educational opportunities and international exchanges for students, teachers, and other professionals, and vocational and career development. Rotary motto is “SERVICE ABOVE SELF”.

Known no better as the father of Rotary Foundation, Arch C Klumphs idea of an endowment fund dedicated to “doing good in the world” initiated The Rotary Foundation (TRF) way back in 1917. His vision and staunch advocacy and with the extraordinary generosity of Rotary members around the world, the fund has become one of the largest humanitarian foundation.
Rotaract Club of Pune Vibrant East, the community based first Rotaract club was chartered on 1st June 2019. The Club comprises of 30 members including most members/students from engineering discipline. The club members are highly motivated and continually engage in several service projects including Blood Donation Camps, Water Conservation Awareness Program, knowledge-sharing events and others.
Two Interact clubs were started in the first year of inception, at two schools in Hadapsar area, namely at –
To enhance the activities of the Interact Club, the school teachers have been urged to become members of our club. This ensures ease in management and coordination of activities within the Interact Clubs. The school has been provided with equipment’s through generous donation from the club. Equipment’s like Sanitary Pad Dispenser Unit and Sanitary Pad Incinerator Unit are installed within the school premises to improvise and ensure regular attendance of girl students, who otherwise, would remain absent from school.
The NGSE program is a short term, tailored designed program for college students and young professionals, until the age of 30 years. Based upon the academic background of the aspirant, the exchange program particularly benefits those who desire to enhance their professional skills on an international platform. The benefits are multifold and we do intend to plan for such an exchange program in the future.
